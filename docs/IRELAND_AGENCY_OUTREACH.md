# Ireland Agency Outreach

Practical workflow for building a small, high-quality list of Ireland-based recruitment agencies for Scout Signal beta outreach.

## Goal

Build a focused list of `20-50` Ireland-based recruitment agencies that are likely to value:

- earlier hiring momentum signals
- better company prioritization
- BD/recruiter workflow support
- startup, growth, engineering, or contract-market visibility

Use published business contact points where possible.

## List Template

Use [IRELAND_AGENCY_OUTREACH_TEMPLATE.csv](/Users/conorwalsh/Downloads/scout-signal/docs/IRELAND_AGENCY_OUTREACH_TEMPLATE.csv).

Recommended fields:

- `agency_name`
- `website`
- `location`
- `niche`
- `public_contact_email`
- `contact_page`
- `source`
- `why_fit`
- `outreach_status`
- `last_contacted_at`
- `notes`

## Good Sources

Start with higher-trust sources first:

- Ireland recruitment/employment association member directories
- agency website contact pages
- LinkedIn company pages
- company "About" or "Contact" pages

Prefer:

- official business emails like `info@`, `hello@`, or team inboxes
- contact forms intended for business enquiries

Avoid building a giant list. Quality matters more than volume.

## Suggested Segments

Useful segments for Scout Signal:

- tech / engineering recruitment
- startup / scale-up hiring
- contract / interim / temp-to-perm
- commercial / sales hiring
- executive search for growth-stage companies

## Outreach Principles

Keep it:

- short
- specific
- business-focused
- feedback-led rather than sales-led

Explain why they were contacted:

- they are an Ireland-based recruitment agency
- they appear to recruit into scaling teams
- Scout Signal may help identify companies showing early hiring momentum

## Offer (Use This Positioning)

Use an outcome-led offer, not a generic beta ask:

- "7-day Pro trial"
- "I set up your top 10 target companies for you"
- "You get one weekly shortlist: who to contact this week"

Primary CTA:

- "Reply with 10 company names and I will set this up this week."

## Primary Email Draft (Outcome-Led)

Subject: 7-day Pro trial for your agency desk

Hi,

I run ScoutSignal and I am offering a small number of Ireland agencies a 7-day Pro trial.

I will set up your top 10 target companies for you and send one shortlist of who to contact this week based on early hiring momentum signals.

If useful, just reply with 10 company names and I will set it up this week.

If you would prefer no follow-up, reply `no` and I will close this out.

Best,
Conor

## Follow-Up (If No Reply, Day 3-4)

Quick follow-up in case this is relevant: I can run a 7-day Pro trial for your desk, set up 10 target companies, and send one shortlist of who looks worth contacting this week.

If you want to try it, reply with 10 company names and I will set it up.

Best,
Conor

## Notes On GDPR / ePrivacy

Keep the process conservative:

- use published business contact points where possible
- avoid collecting unnecessary personal data
- record why each agency was contacted
- keep outreach relevant to their business role
- include a simple opt-out in every message

This is not legal advice, but it is a sensible operating baseline for low-volume B2B beta outreach.

## Suggested Status Values

Use these in `outreach_status`:

- `not_started`
- `queued`
- `sent`
- `replied`
- `interested`
- `not_now`
- `opt_out`

## Recommended First Pass

Start with:

1. `20` agencies max
2. strong niche fit
3. only official contact channels
4. one short follow-up at most unless they engage

The best version of this list is not the biggest one. It is the cleanest one.
