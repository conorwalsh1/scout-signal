# Trial Activation 24h Playbook

Use this immediately after an agency replies "interested".

## Goal

Move from interest to value in under 24 hours:

- collect target company list
- provision access
- deliver first shortlist

## Step 1 - Reply within 2 hours

Send:

Subject: Re: 7-day Pro trial

Thanks - great.  
Send me your top 10 target companies (or 5 if easier), and I will set up the trial today.

I will send your first "who to contact this week" shortlist after setup.

## Step 2 - Setup checklist (same day)

- confirm account email
- confirm plan set to Pro trial
- confirm company targets saved
- verify dashboard shows ranked targets
- verify provenance lines visible for trust

## Step 3 - Deliver first shortlist (same day)

Send 5 rows max:

- company
- why now (what changed)
- evidence source
- suggested outreach window

## Step 4 - Set close expectation early

At the end of the shortlist email include:

"I will check in on day 6 with a simple keep/pause decision."

## Trial success markers

- user logs in at least twice
- user confirms at least 1 signal looked useful
- user asks for additional companies/signals

If no login by day 2: send a short rescue email with one highlighted company insight.
